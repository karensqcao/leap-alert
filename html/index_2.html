Who Will be benefitted?

One of the greatest benefits of technology, is its potential to improve the lives of others, and we sought to accomplish this with our application.

Autism

People on the autism spectrum often show difficulties in verbal forms of communication. LeapAlert gives those on the spectrum an opportunity to gain experience in expressing themselves through body language.

The Blind

Technology is an extremely visual medium, making it very inaccessible to those with visual impairments. Our simplified swipe technology helps remove many of the barriers of visual interfaces.

Childcare

A parents greatest fear is leaving their child alone. LeapAlert helps lessen those fears by giving children a simple and quick way to communicate issues with their parents.

SMS Integration

This prototype our application has automated the task of sending a text message, allowing users to send out text alerts
